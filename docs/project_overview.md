## Project Overview: Student Thesis Management Platform



### Overview

A website platform designed for final-year students completing their graduation thesis, addressing the need for fairness in teamwork grading. The software connects instructors and student groups, allowing faculty to monitor work progress, contribution scores, and task management, ensuring equitable outcomes for every student.

---

### Objectives

* **Ensure Fairness:** Build a transparent evaluation mechanism. Students are required to document their workflow and provide evidence of their results. AI is utilized to provide an overview assessment of student notes and outputs.


* **Support Faculty Management:** Provide tools that help instructors track work progress and manage individual student tasks effectively.


* **Enhance Connectivity:** Create a centralized platform connecting instructors and student groups, facilitating supervision and ensuring the quality of the thesis.



---

### Core Functions

* **Task Management System:** Allows students to create, assign, update status, and set deadlines for all project-related tasks.


* **Contribution Score:** A module developed to measure and display individual contribution points (automatically or semi-automatically) based on work output and peer reviews within the group.


* **Progress Monitoring:** Instructors can view both high-level summaries and detailed progress of each group, including activity history and work logs.


---

### Ideas

* **Backend:** Build tools for the AI so it can execute any user request, while restricted by user type and limited to the scope of the specific website. Currently, the platform consists of two main interfaces—the student/instructor workspace and the login page. For example, a user might want to generate a deadline for their team or draft a report based on their specific results and notes.
